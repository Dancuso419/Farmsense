# FarmSense — Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 2–3 minutes
**Tone:** calm, confident, mission-driven. You're showing a tool that works where nothing else does.

---

## ⚙️ Before you hit record (do this every time)

The laptop is a CPU-only machine, so a cold model is slow. Set the stage first:

1. **Warm the model.** Open the app and send one throwaway question ("hello") and wait for the full reply. This loads Gemma into RAM so on-camera replies start fast. (We set `keep_alive: -1`, so it stays warm.)
2. **Prepare a clear crop photo** on the phone/laptop — ideally a diseased leaf (e.g. cassava mosaic, tomato blight). Have it ready in the file picker location.
3. **Pre-write your demo questions** (below) so you don't fumble typing on camera.
4. **Close RAM hogs** — WSL, VMware, extra browser tabs. More free RAM = faster answers.
5. **Silence notifications** (Windows Focus Assist on, phone on Do Not Disturb).
6. **Recording tip:** if a reply still takes a while, either (a) let it stream — the streaming text actually looks good on camera, or (b) trim/speed-up the "thinking" gap by 2–4× in your editor. Never cut mid-stream; the word-by-word reveal is a selling point.

---

## 🎬 Scene 1 — The Hook (0:00–0:20)

**On screen:** Full-screen the landing page hero (green section, "Your farm's expert, offline").

**Voiceover:**
> "Millions of smallholder farmers lose crops to disease every season — not because there's no cure, but because there's no expert nearby, and often no internet. This is FarmSense: an AI farming assistant that runs completely offline."

**Action:** Slowly scroll the hero so the grass/parallax animation shows.

---

## 🎬 Scene 2 — What it does (0:20–0:40)

**On screen:** Scroll through **How it works** → **Crops** → **Offline/trust** sections.

**Voiceover:**
> "A farmer describes a problem or snaps a photo. FarmSense diagnoses the disease and gives a practical, local treatment — in seconds. It's tuned for the crops of Southeast Nigeria: cassava, yam, maize, tomato, plantain, pepper and more."

**Action:** Pause briefly on the Crops grid, then on the cocoa "Made for where the signal ends" section.

---

## 🎬 Scene 3 — Ask a question (text) (0:40–1:15)

**On screen:** Tap the floating leaf button → chat opens.

**Voiceover:**
> "Let's ask it something real."

**Action:** Type (or tap a suggestion chip):
> *"My cassava leaves have yellow patches and are curling."*

**As it streams in, voiceover:**
> "Notice the answer streams in word by word — and it's short and structured: what the disease likely is, and exactly what to do about it. No jargon."

**Action:** Point out the clean bullet-point treatment. Then send a follow-up to show memory:
> *"Is it safe to eat the tubers?"*

**Voiceover:**
> "And it remembers the conversation, so follow-ups just work."

---

## 🎬 Scene 4 — Diagnose from a photo (1:15–1:45)

**On screen:** Tap the upload button → pick the diseased-leaf photo → preview appears → send.

**Voiceover:**
> "It's not just text. Take a photo of a sick plant, and the same AI looks at the image and diagnoses it visually — the way an extension officer would."

**Action:** Let the diagnosis stream in. Highlight that the image shows in the chat bubble.

---

## 🎬 Scene 5 — Speaks their language (1:45–2:05)

**On screen:** Send a question in Igbo (or Yoruba/Hausa).

**Suggested Igbo prompt:**
> *"Gịnị ka m ga-eme maka ọrịa na-emetụta ọka m?"* (What do I do about the disease affecting my maize?)

**Voiceover:**
> "And the farmer never has to change a setting. Ask in English, Igbo, Yoruba or Hausa — FarmSense detects the language and answers back in the same one."

---

## 🎬 Scene 6 — The offline magic (2:05–2:35)

**On screen:** Show the phone. Then **turn off WiFi / go airplane mode**. Open the installed FarmSense app from the home screen. Ask one more question.

**Voiceover:**
> "Here's the part that matters most. This is running on a laptop over local WiFi — no internet at all. I'll prove it: airplane mode on… and it still works. Nothing leaves the device. No cloud, no accounts, no data collected. The farmer's questions and photos stay private."

**Action:** Show the "Ready · offline" status in the chat header, and the app icon on the phone's home screen.

---

## 🎬 Scene 7 — Close (2:35–2:50)

**On screen:** Back to the landing hero or the CTA band.

**Voiceover:**
> "FarmSense — an expert in every farmer's pocket, online or off. Built with open AI models, so anyone can run it. Thank you."

## 🗣️ Backup demo questions (all fast, on-topic)

- "White powder is on my tomato leaves, what is it?"
- "How do I stop maize stem borers?"
- "My yam is rotting in storage. What can I do?"
- "Best time to plant cassava in the southeast?"

## ⚠️ Don't demo on camera
- Off-topic prompts (it will politely refuse — good for a *separate* "safety" clip, not the main demo).
- The very first cold question (warm it beforehand).
