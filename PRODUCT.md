# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Smallholder farmers in rural Southeast Nigeria — primary users. Low-literacy, low-tech, may type in English, Igbo, Yoruba, or Hausa. Access the app on a phone connected to the host laptop via local WiFi. Agricultural extension officers and farming students as secondary users.

## Product Purpose

FarmSense is an offline-first AI farming assistant. Farmers describe crop symptoms or upload a photo, and the app returns a diagnosis and treatment advice powered by Gemma 4 running locally via Ollama. No internet required after setup. Success: a farmer in a village with no data signal gets an accurate crop disease diagnosis and acts on it before the harvest is lost.

## Brand Personality

Earthy, Bold, Caring — the feel of a knowledgeable farming elder: confident, warm, rooted in the land. Never clinical. Never corporate. Speaks plainly and treats the farmer as capable.

## Anti-references

- Generic agri-tech SaaS: blue gradient dashboards, B2B crop monitoring aesthetics, metric-heavy UI
- Western farming apps: John Deere yellow/green palette, American corn field imagery — foreign and irrelevant to Nigerian farmers
- Government / NGO portals: bloated forms, bureaucratic hierarchy, heavy tables

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — every label, button, and response must be immediately legible to a low-literacy user
2. **Earth before screen** — the palette, texture, and tone should feel like soil and leaves, not software
3. **One task at a time** — the interface never shows more than what the farmer needs right now
4. **Offline-native** — no spinner states that imply cloud dependency; waiting states must feel local and trustworthy
5. **Multilingual-ready** — Gemma auto-detects and replies in English, Igbo, Yoruba, or Hausa (no manual switch); layout must hold when text expands for local-language responses

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Minimum WCAG AA contrast on all text
- Touch targets minimum 48×48px — farmers use phones with working hands
- No reliance on color alone to convey meaning
- Reduced motion support for all transitions
